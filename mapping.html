Moonlight

Video mapping

What was the goal?

During a workshop, we had to choose a music and create a whole video mapping on a model that we had to build. The mapping was done on Touch Designer and the animations on After Effect. We were in a team of 5.

What did I do ?

We imagined a classical concert of the 3rd movement of the moonlight sonata where the pianist is on top of giant stairs in a transparent bubble. I worked on the organisation of the animation and did them on After Effect.
